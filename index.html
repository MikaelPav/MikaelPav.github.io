Our Mission

RKT VENTURES was established on 29 January 1998 by a young man who possessed a high vision. It has been operating for more than 10 years. Our main core businesses are supply manpower, civil works, cleaning, and maintenance services.

In 2007, RKT launched the company motto “TOWARDS ADVANCEMENT” and in 2005, the motto was achieved through the hard work of efficient management to develop the company.
